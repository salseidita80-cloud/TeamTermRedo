Compatibility Score Calculation

How the application mathematically calculates roommate match percentages.

Formula
categoryScore = round((1 - |SalValue - CandidateValue| / 9) × 100)
finalScore = round(weighted average of all category scores)

Each lifestyle answer uses a 1–10 scale. A perfect match in a category receives 100%. The farther apart two answers are, the lower that category score becomes. Scores are then weighted and averaged.
